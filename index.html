FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Personal Relationship Advisory (PRA)?

Personal Relationship Advisory (PRA) is a tailored advisory service focused on nurturing genuine bonds and fostering lifelong fulfillment in personal relationships. Our expert guidance helps individuals navigate various aspects of their personal lives to achieve greater happiness and satisfaction.

What types of services does Priyanka PRA offer?

Priyanka PRA offers a range of services to help individuals build stronger relationships, including Personal Relationship Advisory, Couples Therapy, and Family Counseling. Each service is personalized to address the unique needs and goals of our clients.

How does the advisory process work?

The advisory process begins with an initial consultation to understand your specific challenges and goals. From there, we develop a customized plan tailored to your needs. Throughout the process, we provide ongoing support, guidance, and resources to help you achieve your desired outcomes.

Is Priyanka PRA confidential?

Yes, confidentiality is of utmost importance to us. We adhere to strict privacy policies to ensure that all client information remains confidential and secure.

How long does each advisory session last?

The duration of each advisory session may vary depending on the specific needs and goals of the client. Typically, sessions range from 60 to 90 minutes, but we can adjust the length as needed to ensure thorough and effective support.

What if I'm not satisfied with the advisory services?

Your satisfaction is our top priority. If you're not completely satisfied with our services, please let us know, and we will do our best to address your concerns and make things right.
